VIRGINIA SINCLAIR'S GALLERY



Mom left behind a lot of beautiful artwork, and I developed a scheme for sharing these treasures in case anyone wants them. Shame on me, there has been a 9-year hiatus (it didn't seem that long!).

You are in the 4th round, the NIECES' AND NEPHEWS' round (Rosalie's kids, Jeanette's kids, Juanita's kids, Bob's kids, and Allen's kids). This website has photographs and descriptions of all the pieces that are still available. You will get to keep a painting of your choice. Use this form to select a list of up to four paintings in order of preference so you have an alternate in case someone else picks the same painting as you and you don't wind up getting to keep your first or second or even your third choice.

Please respond by February 14, 2026. If you miss the deadline, there will no doubt be some paintings in the free-for-all round that will happen after other relatives and friends have picked through them.

I can hand-deliver to the Sinclair family reunion, Phoenix, Flagstaff, and Pagosa Springs in May. If none of those hand-delivery options work for you, you have a choice of having the artwork shipped to you or picking it up from Kirsten's house. Shipping cost estimates are given in the description for each piece. Your painting will be professionally packaged and shipped to you via UPS as soon as your shipping payment is received (make checks out to"Kirsten Rosselot" and mail them to Kirsten Rosselot, 5657 Ruthwood Dr, Calabasas, CA 91302). If the actual shipping costs are lower than the estimate on this website, your excess payment will be refunded. There is no guarantee that glass will not break during shipping, but it is not likely to given the care taken in packaging.

The form will allow you to choose fewer paintings than the maximum, but I recommend that you pick four.

You will see that some of the pieces are framed, some are matted, and some are neither matted nor framed. Most are watercolor but there are also some oil, acrylic, and pastel pieces. Dimensions are given for each piece. Some of the matted pieces are in a protective transparent sleeve and they were not removed from the sleeves when photos were taken. Note that some of the pieces have already been hanging and/or done a bit of traveling and there is some wear and tear on some of the frames. If we noticed any damage while preparing the photos, it is mentioned in the description. Some of the pieces were offered for sale at shows and still have price tags on them, ignore those price tags.

Happy viewing!

-Kirsten
