Here are the details on how the artwork is being doled out:

First, my brothers and I will look at the paintings on this website and make a list of the ones we want, putting them in order (1st choice, 2nd choice, etc). The three of us will pick out the ones we want at the same time. If more than one of us wants the same painting, priority will be based on age and preference, so if Rodney and Ivan, for example, both want the same painting as their first choice, the painting will go to Rodney. But if they both want another painting that is Ivan's second choice and Rodney's third choice, the painting will go to Ivan. Each of us will get to keep up to eight of our choices.

Next, Mom's grandchildren will make their choices, and get to keep up to four of their choices. Mom's siblings will be next, with up to two pieces each, then her nieces and nephews with one piece each. In the last round, other relatives and friends will get a chance with one piece each (this round will be first-come first-served, not in order of age). If anything is left after that, it will be open to all on a first-come first-served basis.
